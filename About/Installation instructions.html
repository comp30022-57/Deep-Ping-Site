Installation instructions

  1. Open the Apps Script editor by clicking:

    here

  1. Click the "Deploy" button, then "Test deployments".
  1. From the drop-down, select the latest tagged version. It should look something like this:
  1. Click "Install".
  1. Open your Gmail client. You should see a sidebar like this on the right of your screen:

    If you don't see the side-bar, click the "Show side panel" arrow at the bottom right of the screen:

  1. Click the blue 'D' icon to open Deep Ping.
  1. Deep Ping requires authorization. Click "AUTHORIZE ACCESS".
  1. You will see a warning about Deep Ping being unverified. Click to proceed.
  1. You may also see this page:

    If you do, click "Advanced", then click to proceed.

  1. You will see a list of permissions. Read over the list and click to proceed once you understand and approve.
  1. Deep Ping is installed.
